# Market Research & Analytics — Case Study

Applying market research and analytics to evaluate a new market: **a European SME's entry into Japan.**

A consulting-style analysis that turns a market-intelligence report — *Japanese Consumers' Behavior: By Age & Gender* (EU–Japan Centre / Haghirian, 2021) — into evidence-based marketing strategy, across five tasks: market attractiveness, consumer understanding, segmentation, recommendations, and a critical evaluation of the research itself.

## The recommendation, in one line

Enter Japan as a **premium, authentically-European brand** for the **silver market** (single-person households as the growth hedge); adapt the entire mix to the Japanese bar; enter patiently — and **validate with primary research before committing capital.**
